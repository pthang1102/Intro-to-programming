Sea Turtle

Temperature dictates the sex of baby turtles!!

  • Average Length: 80-115 cm
  • Average Weight: 110-190 kg
  • Average Lifespan: 50-100 years
  • Habitat: Near the coastline and around islands

Sea turtles don't really have a favorite food, though it is commonly believed that they love jellyfish! They have been around for more than 100 million years - even surviving the dinosaurs when they became extinct 65 million years ago. They are considered threatened or endangered. It is estimated that only 1 out of 1000 hatchlings survives to be an adult.
